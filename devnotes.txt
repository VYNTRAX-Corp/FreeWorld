Day 3 of building my own FPS game from scratch 🎮

The game finally looks like a GAME 🔥

VFX pass:
Every bullet now leaves a tracer in the air ✨
Hit an enemy? Blood splatter 🩸
Hit a wall? Sparks fly + bullet hole stays on the surface
Muzzle flash = real point light burst, not a fake sprite
Post-processing turned on — Bloom, Vignette, Chromatic Aberration
The game went from "Unity default grey" to actually cinematic

Enemy AI that actually fights back:
Before today enemies just walked at you and stood still shooting 🤦
Now they:
→ Strafe sideways while firing (hard to hit) ✅
→ Flank you from the side ✅
→ Investigate your last position if you hide ✅
→ Dive for cover when you shoot them ✅
→ Learn from you — the faster you kill, the smarter they get ✅

That last one — the adaptive difficulty — is wild to watch.
No ML, no neural nets, just a rolling kill-speed tracker.
Kill fast = they react faster, flank more, chase harder.
Die = they ease off so it stays fun.

Polish pass:
🔫 Procedural gun models — rifle, pistol & shotgun, zero assets
🎮 Weapon carousel HUD with coloured silhouette icons
🩸 Directional damage indicator — red arrow points to where you got shot from
👣 Enemy footsteps that speed up when they chase you
🧱 Real PBR textures — concrete, brick, wood, metal — all CC0 free

Everything is 100% code, zero bought assets

Day 2 part 2 of building my own FPS game from scratch 🎮

Enemies finally look like actual soldiers 🪖

Built a full procedural humanoid from scratch — head, torso,
arms, legs, hands, boots — no assets, pure code 👀

Then gave them real guns.
Not just "shoot a raycast" — a full shooting system:
burst fire, magazine, reload animations, headshot multiplier,
muzzle flash, impact sparks...

But then I had to fix some nasty bugs to make them actually work —
enemies weren't respawning, the timer was frozen, kill counter stuck at 0...

Turns out ONE component sharing a GameObject with another
was silently destroying the entire game manager every session 💀

Sometimes the hardest bugs have the simplest causes

→ Jointed humanoid body built from primitives ✅
→ Rifle parented to the right forearm ✅
→ Arms raise into aiming pose when attacking ✅
→ Reload animation drops the arm to change mag ✅
→ Headshots deal 2× damage ✅
→ Timer now counts down ✅
→ Enemies spawn in waves ✅
→ Kill counter tracks your score ✅
→ HUD styled like a real FPS ✅

The enemies went from pink balloons to actual armed soldiers 🔫
